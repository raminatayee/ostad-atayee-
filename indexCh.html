Children's Department

The Children's Department offers a range of programs tailored to the developmental needs of young learners. The key areas include:

Learning programs for children

Learning Readiness Program for Grades 1 to 3

Our Educational Readiness Program for grades 1 through 3 focuses on foundational learning and skill development. The curriculum includes:

Literacy: Phonics, reading comprehension, and basic writing skills.

Mathematics: Number recognition, simple arithmetic, and introduction to shapes and patterns.

Art and Creativity: Drawing, painting, and crafts to foster creativity.

Learning Readiness Program for Grades 4 to 9

Our Educational Readiness Program for grades 4 through 9 provides a comprehensive foundation for academic growth. The curriculum includes:

Literacy and Language Arts: Advanced reading comprehension, writing skills, and literary analysis.

Mathematics: Algebra, geometry, and problem-solving techniques.

Social Studies: History, geography, and cultural studies.

Learning Readiness Program for Grades 10 to 12

Our Educational Readiness Program for grades 10 through 12 prepares students for higher education and future careers. The curriculum includes:

Mathematics: Pre-calculus, calculus, and advanced problem-solving.

echnology and Computer Science: Programming, data analysis, and digital tools.

Career and College Readiness: Resume writing, interview skills, and college application processes.

English Readiness Program

Our English Readiness Program provides foundational skills for students to excel in English. The program covers:

Reading: Comprehension, vocabulary, and fluency.

Writing: Grammar, sentence structure, and creative writing.

Speaking: Pronunciation, conversation skills, and public speaking.

Listening: Understanding spoken English and following directions.

Islamic Studies Readiness Program

Our Islamic Studies Readiness Program offers a foundational understanding of Islamic teachings and practices. The program includes:

Quranic Studies: Basic understanding of Quranic verses and memorization

Hadith: Introduction to sayings of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) and their applications.

Fiqh (Islamic Jurisprudence): Basic principles of Islamic law and daily practices.

Aqidah (Islamic Beliefs): Core beliefs and theological concepts in Islam.

Computer Readiness Program

Our Computer Readiness Program provides essential skills for effective use of technology. The program includes:

Basic Computer Skills: Introduction to operating systems, file management, and basic software.

Office Applications: Proficiency in word processing, spreadsheets, and presentations.

Internet and Email: Effective use of web browsers, search engines, and email communication.

Programming Basics: Introduction to coding concepts and basic programming languages.
